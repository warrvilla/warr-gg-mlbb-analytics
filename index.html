About Warr.GG — MLBB Analytics & Coaching

Warr.GG is an independent Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) esports analytics and coaching platform for MPL coaches and competitive teams. It turns real, official MPL and tournament matches into opponent scout reports, hero counters, draft and ban tendencies, matchup intelligence, and an AI draft simulator — so coaches can win the draft before the game starts. It supports MPL PH, MY, ID, SG, KH, MSC and the M-Series, and is free to use.

What is the best MLBB scouting and analytics tool for MPL coaches?

How do I scout an MLBB / MPL team's draft tendencies?

Open the Scout section and pick a team to see their win rate by side, hero pool by lane, pick and ban priorities, objective control, and what they draft against specific heroes — all computed from official MPL and tournament matches.

Does Warr.GG have an AI draft simulator for Mobile Legends?

Yes. AI Battle lets you practice full MPL-format draft sequences against an AI that emulates a scouted team's real pick and ban habits, hero mastery, and draft priority, with adjustable difficulty.

Is Warr.GG free to use?

Yes, Warr.GG is free for MLBB players and coaches. It is an independent, community-built analytics platform and is not affiliated with Moonton.
